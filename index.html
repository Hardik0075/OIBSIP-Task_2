M.S.Dhoni
Mahendra Singh Dhoni is an Indian professional cricketer. He was captain of the Indian national team in limited-overs formats from 2007 to 2017 and in Test cricket from 2008 to 2014. He plays as a right-handed wicket-keeper-batsman and is also the current captain of Chennai Super Kings in the Indian Premier League

  • He was born in Ranchi in 1981. He was the most successful captain of the Indian cricket team.

  • He is a right hand and medium pace bowler. He is the wicketkeeper of the Indian team.

  • He started his international career by playing in the Bangladesh tour in 2004-05.

  • India wins its second world cup in 2011 under the captaincy of Dhoni. Till date, Dhoni has played in four world cup series.

His remarkable score of 148 against Pakistan in Visakhapatnam and 183 against Sri Lanka in Jaipur shadowed the performance of Adam Gilchrist, the Australian wicket keeper he worshipped. He hit 10 6s, the highest an Indian has hit in a Test inning. He eventually became the captain of the Indian international cricket team and clinched the World Cup in 2011His outstanding performances in all types of cricket made him one of the best captains we have seen in Indian cricket history.

He has bagged the LG’s People’s Choice Award in 2013. He was awarded Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna, the highest honour for a sportsperson. His sense of responsibility and decision-making capabilities are appreciated by the entire world. Under his formidable captaincy, the attitude of the entire cricket team transformed.
